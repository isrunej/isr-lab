Research Areas / Bidang Riset

Six Pillars of Inquiry

From intelligent control and agro-robotics to biosignal analysis and digital agriculture — our research bridges engineering, health, and food systems. Dari kendali cerdas dan agro-robotika hingga analisis biosignal dan pertanian digital.

01

🤖

Agro-Industrial Robotics

Design, kinematics, and control of robotic systems for agriculture — harvesting, sorting, and precision spraying robots.

Desain, kinematika, dan kendali robot untuk pertanian agro-industri Indonesia.

KinematicsPath PlanningManipulation

02

🧠

Intelligent Control Systems

AI-augmented control including fuzzy logic, neural network controllers, and adaptive systems for nonlinear dynamics.

Sistem kendali berbasis AI: fuzzy, neural network, dan adaptif untuk dinamika nonlinier.

Fuzzy PIDAdaptiveRL Control

03

👁️

Machine Perception

Computer vision and sensor fusion for agricultural monitoring — disease detection, yield estimation, and navigation.

Visi komputer dan sensor fusion untuk monitoring pertanian cerdas berbasis AI.

Deep LearningSensor FusionEdge AI

04

📡

Smart Instrumentation & IoT

IoT-enabled sensor networks, wireless communication, and precision measurement for environmental and agricultural data.

Jaringan sensor IoT, komunikasi nirkabel, dan instrumentasi presisi untuk data pertanian.

IoTEdge AIWireless

05

🔬

Biosignal & Assistive Tech

Brain-computer interfaces, myoelectric control, and biosignal pattern recognition for assistive devices in health and rehabilitation.

BCI, kendali mioelektrik, dan pengenalan pola biosignal untuk alat bantu rehabilitasi.

BCIMyoelectricRehab Tech

06

🌾

Digital Agriculture Systems

End-to-end integrated systems combining robotics, AI, and IoT for smart farming at agro-industrial scale in Indonesia.

Sistem terpadu robotika, AI, dan IoT untuk smart farming skala agro-industri nasional.

Digital TwinSCADADSS
Lab Roadmap

Strategic Development
Peta Jalan Pengembangan Lab 2022–2030

A phased plan from foundational infrastructure to a nationally recognized center of excellence in intelligent agro-industrial systems.

PHASE 01

2022–23

✓ Completed

Foundation & Infrastructure

Membangun identitas lab, pengadaan peralatan inti, dan kompetensi riset awal.

  • Lab facility di Gedung CDAST Lt. 7
  • Robot prototype pertama
  • Publikasi awal (S1/S2 level)
  • Pendanaan riset internal UNEJ

PHASE 02

2024–25

⬤ Ongoing

Research Growth & Collaboration

Meningkatkan output publikasi dan membangun kolaborasi strategis nasional.

  • Target Sinta 1–2 journals
  • MoU kolaborasi industri
  • Hibah kompetitif nasional
  • Persiapan program S3

PHASE 03

2026–28

Planned

S3 Launch & International Reach

Membuka program doktoral dan mencapai visibilitas internasional.

  • Program S3 Teknik Elektro UNEJ
  • IEEE/Scopus Q1–Q2 papers
  • Konferensi internasional
  • Student exchange program

2028–30

Planned

Academic Programs / Program Studi

Study & Research Opportunities

ISR Lab actively supports S1 and S2 research. Our doctoral program is on the horizon — join us now and grow with the lab. ISR Lab mendukung riset S1 dan S2. Program S3 sedang dalam persiapan — bergabunglah sekarang.

S1

Sarjana / Bachelor

Undergraduate Research Track

Undergraduate students from any faculty can join ISR Lab as research assistants, contributing to active projects under faculty supervision. Skripsi aligned with lab themes is strongly encouraged.

Mahasiswa S1 dari berbagai prodi dapat bergabung sebagai asisten riset di bawah bimbingan dosen. Penelitian skripsi yang selaras dengan tema lab sangat dianjurkan.

Available Research Tracks

  • Robotic arm design & forward kinematics
  • IoT-based smart sensing systems
  • Computer vision for agricultural inspection
  • Biosignal processing for assistive devices
S2

Magister / Master

Graduate Research Program

Graduate students are full lab members with access to all research facilities, publication support, and industry collaboration opportunities. Thesis topics are co-developed with the Head of Lab.

Mahasiswa S2 adalah anggota penuh lab dengan akses fasilitas riset lengkap, dukungan publikasi, dan kolaborasi industri. Topik tesis dikembangkan bersama kepala lab.

Featured Thesis Topics

  • Adaptive fuzzy control for agro-robotic systems
  • Efficient CNN for edge agricultural AI
  • Multi-sensor fusion for precision farming
  • BCI-based assistive device for rehabilitation
