Research Areas / Bidang Riset

Six Pillars of Inquiry
Enam Pilar Penelitian Unggulan

From foundational control theory to applied agro-industrial systems — our research targets real challenges across the intelligent systems spectrum.
Dari teori kendali dasar hingga sistem agro-industri terapan.

01

🤖

Agro-Industrial Robotics

Design, kinematics, and control of robotic systems for agriculture — harvesting, sorting, and precision spraying robots.

Desain, kinematika, dan kendali robot untuk pertanian agro-industri Indonesia.

KinematicsPath PlanningManipulation

🧠

Intelligent Control Systems

AI-augmented control including fuzzy logic, neural network controllers, and adaptive systems for nonlinear dynamics.

Sistem kendali berbasis AI: fuzzy, neural network, dan adaptif untuk dinamika nonlinier.

Fuzzy PIDAdaptiveRL Control

👁️

Visi komputer dan sensor fusion untuk monitoring pertanian cerdas berbasis AI.

Efficient Deep Learning

Peta Jalan Pengembangan Lab 2022–2030

A phased growth plan from foundational infrastructure to a nationally recognized center of excellence in intelligent agro-industrial systems.

2022–23

✓ Completed

Foundation & Infrastructure

Membangun identitas lab, pengadaan peralatan inti, dan kompetensi riset awal.

  • Lab facility & equipment setup
  • Robot prototype pertama
  • Publikasi awal (S1/S2 level)
  • Pendanaan riset internal UNEJ

PHASE 02

2024–25

⬤ Ongoing

Research Growth & Collaboration

Meningkatkan output publikasi dan membangun kolaborasi strategis nasional.

  • Target Sinta 1–2 journals
  • MoU kolaborasi industri
  • Hibah kompetitif nasional
  • Persiapan program S3

PHASE 03

2026–28

Planned

Membuka program doktoral dan mencapai visibilitas internasional.

  • Program S3 Teknik Elektro

Academic Programs / Program Studi

Study & Research
Opportunities

ISR Lab actively supports undergraduate and graduate research. Our doctoral program is on the horizon — join us now and grow with the lab.
ISR Lab mendukung riset S1 dan S2. Program S3 sedang dalam persiapan — bergabunglah sekarang dan berkembang bersama lab.

S1

Sarjana Teknik Elektro

Bachelor of Electrical Engineering

Undergraduate students can join ISR Lab as research assistants, working on lab projects under faculty supervision. Thesis research aligned with lab themes is strongly encouraged.

Mahasiswa S1 dapat bergabung sebagai asisten riset, mengerjakan proyek lab di bawah bimbingan dosen. Penelitian skripsi yang selaras dengan tema lab sangat dianjurkan.

Available Research Tracks

  • Robotic arm design & forward kinematics
  • IoT-based smart sensing systems
  • Computer vision for agricultural inspection
  • Embedded systems & control implementation
S2

Magister Teknik Elektro

Master of Electrical Engineering

Graduate students are full lab members with access to all research facilities, publication support, and potential industry collaborations. Thesis topics are co-developed with the PI.

Mahasiswa S2 adalah anggota penuh lab dengan akses fasilitas riset lengkap, dukungan publikasi, dan kolaborasi industri. Topik tesis dikembangkan bersama PI.

Featured Thesis Topics

  • Adaptive fuzzy control for agro-robotic systems
  • Efficient CNN for edge agricultural AI
  • Multi-sensor fusion for precision farming
  • Digital twin for industrial plant control
